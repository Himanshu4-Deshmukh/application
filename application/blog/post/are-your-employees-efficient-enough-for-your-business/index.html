Are your employees efficient enough for your business?

An efficient employee helps to produce better products and this leads to business growth. Get an amazing solution for monitoring your field employees with TaskEye.

20 December, 2019 Field Employee Monitoring

An efficient employee can help any business to be productive. Every company needs to understand how their employees are working. But when employees are working on the field or travelling, it becomes difficult for their performance. 

You can achieve a lot by just analyzing employees’ work. As your employees are the ones who help you achieve business growth. 

Let’s take a few examples to understand the importance of monitoring each employee's work:

  • Jack has been wasting 2 hrs from 8hrs of his work-time. You have been paying him according to the total working hours. Now other employees also have started wasting their time seeing Jack. This leads to your loss as they are paid for the total work time. By monitoring their performance you will know the exact working hours and you will be able to take steps as required on time.
  • Amy is assigned three tasks. She chooses to do the work which has a lesser priority. When you will have the exact details of the task, she is working on the real-time. You can stop her and inform her regarding the priority. Thus, you will be able to reduce human error.

Analyzing the performance of the employee in the office and on-field are two different tasks. Here we will focus on how can you efficiently monitor the employees on the field.

TaskEye at help...

TaskEye is field employee monitoring system that takes care of employees' performance, task and security. It has a platform which helps the manager and the employee at the same time. Refer blog “Field Employee Monitoring with TaskEye” for knowing TaskEye in a better way.

Here we will see different real-time reports which help to analyze the employee’s performance.

Attendance

Employees at different task location cannot always come to the office for marking their attendance. So TaskEye enables to mark attendance in the app from anywhere. These attendances are collected to create daily reports. The reports of daily, weekly, monthly and custom range can be seen here. Thus, you will have details regarding absent, present, leave, holiday and week off of each employee.

Employee Performance

The performance report is an informative report which gives an overview of each efficiency of completing the task. Here you will get detail of task completed on time, completed with delay, missed task, number of allocated task and average delay duration. You will get real-time updates of every task thus this you will be able to monitor the employee's performance easily.

Employee Status

The employee status provides detail of employee along with his work status. You will get information about his date of birth, employee work type, HES ID, HES expiry date and status with date and time. The status informs you if the employee is on-field, off-field or inactive.

Work Hour

The work hour, as its name suggests helps to know the working hour of each employee. Get employees estimated work hours, actual work hours, duration difference, number of times early on duty, number of times early off duty and number of time late off duty. These details give you a clear view of the working hour.

Employee Task History

This report helps to view the task history of each employee. It efficiently reports the total task given to an employee, the number of the completed task and missed tasks. Thus, not only the daily updates but you will also have control on the previous task of employees.

Closure...

The analyzing of the resource is important for any industry and the same is for the employee. Be connected to us to get more information on TaskEye and other upcoming technologies and features.
