Uffizio is pleased to announce that Toptraking has become our partner now. We look forward to work together.

A big congratulations to connect with us.

#401 #One more device has been successfully integrated in our platform. And I’m glad to inform you that our tracking software is now compatible with Toptraking devices too.

Toptraking

Toptraking is a company specialising in wireless location technology (GPS), and fleet management service. Toptraking provides technology solutions designed to give managers a real-time understanding of the behavior and performance of their vehicles, assets, mobile workforce and consumers

List of Toptraking devices integrated with Uffizio platform

1) TP 07S
TP-07S. This device is a waterproof IPX5, innovative miniature size personal remote positioning device. It’s for monitoring and protecting people and property. It can be used in all walks of life from traveling lone workers and mobile nursing staff to children and the elderly.

2) TP-200
TP-200 device is a waterproof IPX6, innovative miniature size pet remote positioning device with built-in U-blox GPS and GSM/GPRS technology. It’s for monitoring and protecting your pet.
