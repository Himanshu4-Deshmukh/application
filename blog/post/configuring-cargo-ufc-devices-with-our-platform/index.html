31 January, 2019 Technological Partner

One more leading company named Cargo UFC become our technological partner who is one of the largest manufacturers of GPS hardware devices in European market.

Also, Cargo UFC model range represented by six creative GPS trackers.

That devices has a new high-precision GPS / Glonass tracker.

Trackers which is manufactures by Cargo is work batter, more productive and more stable predecessors.

Glimpse of Cargo UFC Devices

1) Cargo Pro 2

Modified nodes of the previous model, significantly improved GPS / Glonass receiver. Also, solving all your problems like field of monitoring for passenger and freight vehicles.

Features  

  • 2 SIM
  • RS485, 1-WIRE, DIN, AIN 
  • Built-in battery and antenna
  • Slave. voltage 6-50B 

2) Cargo Light 2

It is an easy-to-operate model which allow you to organize quickly monitoring of your transportation.

Enhance and eliminate inaccuracies of the previous model as well as add new features to the improved GPS receiver.

Features 

  • 2 SIM
  •  DIN, AIN 
  • Built-in battery and antenna
  • Slave. voltage 6-50B

3) Cargo Pro 2 EXT

All inaccuracies of the previous model were improved and eliminated, new functions were introduced.
The Cargo Pro 2 Ext model has significantly improved GPS / Glonass receiver. Also, solving your all problems in the field of monitoring for vehicles like cars, bike and trucks.

Features 

  • 2 SIM 
  • RS485, 1-WIRE, DIN, AIN 
  • Built-in battery 
  • External GPS / GNSS ant. 
  • Slave. voltage 6-50B 

4) Cargo Light 2 EXT

Cargo Light 2 Ext ia same like Cargo Pro 2 Ext. In additional, some of the new features are implemented which makes it possible to obtain more accurate data.

Features

  •  2 SIM 
  • DIN, AIN 
  • Built-in battery 
  • External GPS / GNSS ant. 
  • Slave. voltage 6-50B

5) Cargo SPY

A standalone GPS tracker - Cargo SPY for tracking the movement of vehicles.
Tracker is equipped with its own 5000 mAh rechargeable battery (CarGo Spy Plus - 7000 mAh).

Features 

  • Smart location, smart roaming 
  • Magnet IP78 
  • Built-in 5000mAh battery 
  • Slave. voltage 6.5-50B 
  • Nom. voltage 12B

6) Cargo Mini 2

It is a compact device for tracking vehicles from the leading European market of GPS monitoring equipment.

Tracker have a high level of performance and functioning.

It has a low cost due to the lack of a built-in battery and RS-485 interface.

Features

  • 2 SIM
  • Bluetooth 
  • DIN, AIN 
  • Slave. voltage 6-50B

7) Cargo UNIT

Ukrainian manufacturer of devices for tracking the movement of objects presents to your attention a self-contained personal GPS tracker - Cargo Unit.

The Cargo Unit was designed to track the movements of people, vehicles and other objects

Features

  • 2 SIM 
  • Bluetooth 
  • Slave. voltage 4,5-16B
  •  Nom. voltage 5V
