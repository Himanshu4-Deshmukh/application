In this telematics industry, number of time I have seen the people are not ready or can’t afford the price of vehicle tracking software.

If you think it’s right, then you need to think twice!

Vehicle tracking software is not only track your vehicle but all the necessary information about your fleet. Like, real-time vehicle tracking, monitoring driving behaviour, multiple analytics reports, multiple chart that represent vehicle status etc…

Here are some myths for not taking Vehicle Tracking Software

  • Vehicle tracking software is only used for tracking your vehicle location
  • We can’t afford, It’s too costly
  • Vehicle tracking system build for only Big scale company
  • Difficult to Install, Setup and Use
  • We manage all vehicle without software

Have a look our pricing plan which is for both the users who are interested in Recurring and One-Time investment. We can assure, that our Saas service price in one of the lowest on the market.

Flexible VTS pricing plan, you decide which suits you best.

Our pricing model is useful for both users who are interested in Pay One time use lifetime as well as recurring.

1) Recurring
We have two types in recurring. Monthly or Yearly.

In a monthly basis, you can pay $1 per month per unit. That means you have to pay every month.

 

In yearly basis, you can pay $6 per year. That means you have to pay only one time in a year.

 

 

 

2) Lifetime

In this pricing model, you have to pay one time and use lifetime just $15.
